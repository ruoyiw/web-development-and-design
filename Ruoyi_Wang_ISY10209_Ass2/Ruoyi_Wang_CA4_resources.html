Environmental Authoritative Website

EE-Link
EE-Link is a environmental education resources search platform. It as a global environmental education resources online supermarket, can classify and retrieval environmental education organizations and projects of different countries easily and quickly.
US EPA Acid Rain Program
An excellent reference on acid rain and the components of pollution.
Water Environment Federation
This site tells about water resources, providing public information about water and the pollution of it.
International Ozone Association
An organization dating back to 1973 that covers a variety of ozone-related issues.
Hazardous Substance Research Center
A US-based organization that does research on hazardous waste, providing a publication and annual reports.
Conservation International
Provides a wonderful multimedia experience, great for kids and young adults who want to explore the natural world from their computer.

Students Environmental Conservation Organisations

Sierra Student Coalition
Sierra Student Coalition (SSC) was founded in 1991, is a branch of the famous American folk environmental protection organization--Sierra Club. SSC is now the largest student environmental protection network which is managed by students.
Student Environmental Action Coalition
Student Environmental Action Coalition(SEAC) was founded in 1998 that consist of the United States, Canada and hundreds of middle school and university group. In local area, you can join the alliance and other young volunteers to participate in environmental protection activities together.
The Center for Environmental Citizenship
The Center for Environmental Citizenship(CEC) is an Environmental group that was found by college students in the United States in 1992, it is a comprehensive website dedicated to youth in environmental leadership roles. CEC aims to develop education, training and the diversity of national youth environmental protection talents network projects to protect the environment.
Youth for Environmental Sanity
The young sponsors and 12-25 years old young people of Youth for Environmental Sanity(YES!) joined in the summer training camp, and organized international students environment meeting.YES was founded in 1990, the goal of it is to organize, support and encourage young people to fight for a fair, peaceful and eco-friendly society.
Campus Ecology
Campus Ecology is a campus environmental protection project which has carried out by wild animal league(National Wildlife Foundation)more than 10 years, its purpose is to convert the American university Campus into an ecological sustainable demonstration community, and to cultivate a new generation of environmental protection talents.

Articles and Media about Global Environment Issues

China Dialogue
You can find and read various topics about international environmental issues, including articles, blogs, books and reports. It is a bilingual website where China and the world discuss the environment.
Green and Environmental Video
There are lots of videos about green and environment classified as different kinds of categories, such as animal protection, fishing, green technology, travel, TV show and so on. They provide an interesting and vivid way to strengthen the awareness of visitors for protecting our environment.
