Changes Made to My Portfolio

There are three changes made to the portfolio that according to some issues on Web:

  1. Australian Copyright Council Information Sheet G079v06 Fair Dealing (2012) states "There are no general provisions in the Copyright Act allowing people to use copyright material with permission for 'personal use'", hence changed the porfolio logo, navigation button background images and King's Music Store logo copied from Google to ones that were produced by myself in a photo editing program.

    http://www.copyright.org.au/

  2. Ishida (2006) in Practical & Cultural Issues in Designing International Web Sites said:

    "A way to approach shallow background is to use a graphic that is three or four lines deep behind the title. By attaching the graphic using the CSS background property, only the amount needed to view the title will actually be shown."
    http://www.w3.org/2006/Talks/fundamentos-Web-ri/slides/Slide0010.html

    Therefore added "top" to CSS background property for page header (logo selector) in order to make the background image more deeper.
